Elec & Me

An Augmented Reality(AR) game for kids of age 6-9 to teach elements and theories of Electricity in a fun and exciting experience.

Elec & Me is a game for iOS devices that is under development using ARKit, SceneKit and SpriteKit. This app detects your flat surfaces where player can place the virtual items on. The goal of the game is to teach players the science of electricity by exploring the functionality of different commonly used appliances at home such as Lamp, and TV. The difficulty of the game increases based on prior theories described in a simple puzzle format.
