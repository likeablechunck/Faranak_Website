Summer 2017

Unity Developer, Designer

:Face

Summer school can be hectic but for me, it was fun! Why? Because I had a chance to work on an app that required understanding new libraries and techniques I have never used. :Face is an AR app to place twenty 2D masks that follow your facial points such as: eyebrows, lips, chin and cheeks. For this app, I researched a lot to be able to find a library that can help up calculate and estimate the head pose. Since we wanted to use Unity3D, OpenCV was a great library to choose from. End result is a group of fun and diverse masks that that can be placed on face for iOS devices that was submitted to App Store.
