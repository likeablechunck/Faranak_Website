My Story

A creative & geek girl who enjoys learning something new everyday

Thanks for visiting my website! I am a MFA student from Academy of Art University, San Francisco majoring in Game Design. My B.S. is from UC Davis as an Electrical Engineer and I have a minor in Technology Management. As a creative and technical person, I set my limits in sky! I started this journey to be able to create games that are unique in their stories, gameplay and in general, the boundle between the game and the player. I have pushed my boundries to learn whatever I need, to be able to communicate my ideas into a polished prototype.

Software I have used so far

During the past 2 years, I have used multiple software in order to communicate and implement my designs. The software such as : Adobe Photoshop, Balsamiq, Unity 3D, Unreal4, Xcode are just few of the major ones that I constantly used.

I have used GitHub for most of my major projects to be able to keep track of our changes we made throughout the project lifecycle. In addition, I have scripted all the projects I was working on with C# in Unity 3D.

For my thesis project, it's under the development and I used SceneKit, SpriteKit and ARKit for iOS devices that run on iOS11 and I am implementing my game in Swift3.

My Thesis

Back in Fall 2016, I researched a lot to be able to narrow down my ideas into something I wanted to present as my thesis project. I wanted to pick a subject that has meaning to me and it personally somehow happened to me that I can share with others. This was how I chose an educational game to teach kids basic theories of Electricity. This topic partially was part of me as an Electrical Engineer. It also brought into my attention that most students have struggles with STEM subjects. So why not make these frightening concepts in a fun and experimental app that kids as young as 6 years old can relate to it. This was how story of Elec & Me shaped and you can check my progress on making this awesome game on my Blog.
